Our Mission

We are group of four programmers who collaberated together and created a full-functioning and usable web development framework for non-profit organizations. The framework that our team built have all components to create any non-profit website with so many purposes in mind from international development, animal welfare, public charities, private foundations to museums.

We choose to help nonprofit organizations built their online foundations and proceed their causes using our great web framework because we built with aspects of nonprofits that is accountability, trustworthiness, honesty, and openness to every person who has invested time, money, and faith into the organization.

We trully believe that this framework have been including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, plugins, Responsive Web Design, creative style and more. The completed team-based project is a culmination of all modern styles and functionality to make sure help nonprofit organizations focus on their mission and raise more money for the organization.
